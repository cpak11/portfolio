Projects

Core News Daily

Core News Daily is a News Hub that provides a one stop shop for all major news in one url. instead of jumping from site to site, just stop here for all the breaking news you need for the day. Updated every time the news updates.

Utilizes the Newsapi to gather the most recent news from over 25 news outlets and sources. Built in AngularJS.

Quo Vadis

A small scale full stack site developed with a small scale ministry in mind. The idea was to be used in outreach and provide information and build upon a culture that we have created for nearly 4 years of service.

Built using the SANE stack utilizing mostly AngularJS along with NodeJS, ExpressJS, Postgres, and SQL handling the back-end of the site.
